Last Updated: February 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to West Georgia TV. We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our weather services:

  • Device information (browser type, operating system)

  • Usage data (how you interact with our services)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide accurate weather information for your area
  • Improve our services
  • Maintain and monitor the performance of our website
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience

4. Data Protection

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Your data is encrypted during transmission and stored securely.

5. Third-Party Services

We utilize third-party services for weather data and maps. These services may have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing their policies:

  • National Weather Service
  • Storm Prediction Center
  • Weather Radar Providers

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Opt out of location-based services
  • Request deletion of your data
